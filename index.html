How to Play

DECK AND THE DATE

Every single day you get 16 cards randomly shuffled.

Every day will be same for everyone so you can go back and play previous days. Use this section to pick a date. If you don't pick a date it will default to today's game!

THE CARDS

Every single day your deck will consist of:

  • F

    5 F's

  • O

    6 O's

  • X

    5 X's

Choose a Card to play

Choose a card by clicking on it. The dancing card is the one you chose.

  • group_work

Click on an empty grid space to move the card to that space. If you want to de-select that one, choose another card or just click on it once again.
